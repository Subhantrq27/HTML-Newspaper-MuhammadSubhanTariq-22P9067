President Ayub's Address

In a historic radio broadcast this morning, Field Marshal Ayub Khan announced that India has committed an act of war. He declared: "We are at war." The President appealed to the nation to stand united against the aggression.

The Air War Details

The Pakistan Air Force (PAF) has struck back with full force. In the initial aerial engagements, PAF eagles have successfully targeted enemy aircraft.

Battle Summary:

  • 22 Indian planes destroyed in air combat.
  • Lahore sector attack successfully halted.
  • Emergency declared throughout the country.
  • Civilians are donating blood for the soldiers.

International Reaction

The United Nations Security Council has called for an immediate ceasefire. Meanwhile, friendly nations have expressed deep concern over the escalation initiated by India.
